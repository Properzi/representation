\subsection*{Ito's theorem}

The theorem states that if $\chi$ is an irreducible character
of a finite group $G$, then $\chi(1)$ divides 
$(G:A)$ for every normal abelian subgroup $A$ of $G$. 
See \cite[\S8.1]{MR0450380}.
